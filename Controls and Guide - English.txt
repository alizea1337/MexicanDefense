Keybindings - Trump:

B - Buildingmode
RMB - Move  (By Clicking)

Keybindings - Tower:

When you have build 1 Defense Tower you can move it and adjust its Place with W A S D

W - Up
A - Left
S - Down
D - Right

You can ONLY move the LAST build Tower!
